Vegeta: The Saiyan Prince

Dragonball isn't known for having the best characters. At best, they serve to move the plot forward to a fight. At worst, they distract from the themes of the show. Vegeta is the exception. Seemingly in the background until it takes center stage in the Majin Buu arc, Vegeta changes from a bloodthirsty tyrant to a man ashamed of his own morality.

Here are some of my favorite quotes with a short description why I like them.

"You're better than me Kakarot, you are number one!"
The absolute culimation of Vegeta's character development. Moving past his pride, and accepting that his rival is stronger at the end of the series.

"You may have invaded my mind and my body, but there's one thing a Saiyan always keeps. HIS PRIDE!"
After being taken over via mind control, Vegeta regains his senses. The delivery of the line is 10/10.

"Trunks, Bulma... I do this for you. And yes, even for you Kakarot."
Vegeta sacrifices himself to save the world. For the first time, as piccolo said, he was fighting for someone other than himself.

"LET ME ASK YOU, DOES A MACHINE LIKE YOURSELF EVER EXPERIENCE FEAR?"
One of Vegeta's biggest moments. After transforming as a super saiyan on screen for the first time, he absolutely dominates a seemingly unbeatable opponent with an incredible finisher.

"I swear on my pride, I'll buy you one minute!"
Against a foe much stronger than himself, Vegeta promises to give Goku a minute to charge up.
